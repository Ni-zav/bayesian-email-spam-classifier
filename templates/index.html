How It Works

This spam classifier uses Bayes' Theorem, a fundamental concept in probability theory and statistics. Here's how it works:

  1. The classifier analyzes patterns in known spam and non-spam emails.
  2. It calculates the probability of specific words appearing in spam versus legitimate emails.
  3. When analyzing a new email, it uses these probabilities to make a prediction.

The formula used is:

P(Spam|Words) = P(Words|Spam) Ã— P(Spam) / P(Words)
